Character Reveal

Every letter carries weight.

Characters cascade into view with rotational depth, creating a sense of dimensionality that flat fades can never achieve. The stagger timing is crucial — too fast feels frantic, too slow feels sluggish.

Word Reveal

Words emerge from silence into meaning.

When words arrive as units, they carry more semantic weight. The reader processes clusters of meaning rather than individual glyphs, creating a rhythm that mirrors natural speech patterns.
