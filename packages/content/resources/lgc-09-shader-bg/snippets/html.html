Demo 09

Shader Background

A full-screen GLSL fragment shader creating flowing aurora bands with layered simplex noise. Move your mouse to influence the flow.

Simplex Noise

Multiple octaves of noise sampled at different scales create organic, flowing movement without repetition.

Mouse Reactive

Cursor position is passed as a uniform, subtly bending the noise field toward the mouse.

Full-Screen Quad

A single plane fills the viewport. All visual complexity lives in the fragment shader — zero geometry.
