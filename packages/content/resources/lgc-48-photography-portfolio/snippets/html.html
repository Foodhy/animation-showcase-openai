About

Quietly
observant.

Elara Voss is a documentary and architectural photographer based between Berlin and Tokyo. Her work explores the tension between built environments and the people who inhabit them.

Exhibited at the Foam Museum Amsterdam, Rencontres d'Arles, and the Tokyo Photographic Art Museum. Published in Aperture, Magnum Photos, and The Guardian Weekend Magazine.
